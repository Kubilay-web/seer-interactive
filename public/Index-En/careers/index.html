    Benefits Build Around You
    Financial Transparency

    Every month, we share revenue, margins and goals with the whole company for full transparency. We average 4% annual salary growth.

    Paid Parental Leave

    We offer 12 weeks of fully-paid leave to all parents welcoming a new child. You’ll also have a phased return to work period following your leave for a more supported transition.

    Unlimited PTO

    We insist that all employees take a minimum of 3 weeks of PTO per year. With paid holidays on top of that, you’re looking at around 6 weeks to do whatever makes you you.

    Unique-to-Seer Benefits

    Little things go a long way to making people feel seen. From Mother’s Day flowers to Father’s Day baskets, to employee appreciation days and more, we recognize that our power is our people.

    People > Profits

    No one here makes less than $65k per year. We reinvest all profits back into the company and have an incredible system for profit sharing.

    Wellness for You

    We’ll cover a % of medical premiums and deductibles and give you a $500 wellness budget to keep you and your family safe and healthy.

    Remote-First

    We’re a remote-first company, which means you work wherever you want and on whatever schedule works best for you and your team.

    Plan for Retirement

    We match your 401K contribution up to 3% of your paycheck. Want to save more? We’ll match 50% of the following 2%.
